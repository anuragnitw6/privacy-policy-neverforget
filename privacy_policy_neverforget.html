Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 10 July 2026

Thank you for using NeverForget. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles your information.

Information We Collect

NeverForget does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to our servers.

Local Data Storage

All checklists, trips, reminders, and other information are stored locally on your device. The data never leaves your phone unless you choose to back it up using your own device backup service.

Permissions

The application only requests permissions that are required for its core functionality. The app does not access your contacts, microphone, camera, or location unless future versions explicitly request your permission.

Internet Connection

The current version of NeverForget works completely offline and does not require an internet connection.

Third-Party Services

This version of the application does not use third-party analytics, advertising services, or cloud storage.

Children's Privacy

NeverForget is not directed toward children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
